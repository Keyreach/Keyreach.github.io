INSTRUCTION MANUAL
What is the objective of game? Conquer your system or at least defeat AI rivals. How to conquer system? Send fleets to other planets. Bigger fleet will win. Remaining fleet will be garrisoned. How is fleet producing? Every planet is producing some amount of ships per turn. How to send fleet on enemy planet? Click on "Send" button of your planet, then on "target" button of target planet, enter amount of ships and that's all.
